About the Data:
The data is taken from onthesnow.com snow reports. For most mountains, it starts on December 27th 2020; however for some mountains it starts on January 10th 2021. The data covers the mountains listed on EPIC's and IKON's website. This page is not affiliated with EPIC pass, IKON pass or onthesnow.com .
